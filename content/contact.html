If you like to contact us, please don't hesitate. We like to know about your projects that are using (parts of) the Ibis project. In fact we're very interested in what you're doing with using the Ibis software, because the more users we have, the more funding we get and the more people are working on improving and maintaining Ibis. So please let us know!
