Ibis Tutorial and Hands-on Session

Monday November 27th 2006
First day of the GRIDs@Work week.

Organizers, Speakers and Tutors:

Rob van Nieuwpoort, Niels Drost and Jason Maassen

Objective:

Slide presentations followed by demonstrations and a guided hands-on programming session. At the end of the day, participants should be able to develop and deploy applications using the JavaGAT, Zorilla, and the Ibis Communication Library.

The subjects to be covered include:

* The JavaGAT
* The Ibis Communication Library
* Zorilla

Participants will have the freedom to choose the features and the applications most significant to them. Full beginners as well as users with a parallel programming experience are welcome.

The tutorial attendees should bring a laptop for actually programming and deploying on the Grid.

More information on JavaGAT, Zorilla, and Ibis can be found on the Ibis project webpage: http://www.cs.vu.nl/ibis
